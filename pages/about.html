Our Story

Built on the Desert Floor

Ground Effects LLC has been crafting custom pools and spas in the Tri-State area for years. We know this landscape — and we know how to build something that lasts in it.

Who We Are

Craftsmen First. Contractors Second.

Ground Effects LLC was founded on a simple belief: that a swimming pool should be engineered as precisely as it is beautiful. We're not a high-volume operation pushing cookie-cutter installs — we take on the projects that demand real skill.

Based in Fort Mohave, we serve homeowners across Bullhead City, Kingman, Lake Havasu City, and across the river into Laughlin, Nevada. The Mojave Desert climate demands durable materials, thoughtful drainage, and finishes that hold up under extreme heat — we design for all of it.

Every project is managed by our own crew from excavation to final finish. No subcontracting the hard parts. No surprises on the back end.
